What is a Leave Application?

A leave application is a formal written request submitted by an employee or student to their employer or principal to seek approval for a temporary absence from work or school. The reason for leave can vary and it may include personal reasons, health issues, family emergencies, vacation, or other situations that require employee or student.

Things to Mention in a Leave Application

  • Start and end dates of the requested leave
  • The reason for the leave
  • Additional information that may be relevant
  • Your details (name, roll number, contact details, signature, address, etc.)

Types of Leave Applications

Leave applications can be categorized into various types. Here are some common types of leave application:-

1. Sick Leave Application

It's used when an employee or student needs time off due to health-related issues. It may require a medical certificate or doctor's note (it depends on company or school rules).

2. Vacation Leave Application

It's requested when an employee wants to take time off for a holiday, relaxation or travel.

3. Maternity/Paternity Leave Application

It is submitted by mothers or fathers seeking time off for the birth of a child. Typically, maternity and paternity leave duration and eligibility depends on company policies and legal regulations.

4. Family Emergency Leave Application

It's requested when an employee needs leave for family emergency. In this application, details of the emergency may be required for approval.

5. Special Leave Application

It's submitted when an employee or student needs time off for a special reason.
